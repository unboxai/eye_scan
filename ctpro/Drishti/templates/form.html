What is Cataract?

Cataract is a common eye condition that affects millions of people worldwide. It is characterized by the clouding of the natural lens of the eye, leading to a progressive loss of vision. Cataracts can occur due to various factors, including aging, exposure to UV radiation, certain medical conditions (such as diabetes), genetic predisposition, and lifestyle choices like smoking and excessive alcohol consumption. For more information on Cataract and what to do in case of a detection, visit here.

Disclaimer: This application uses a Deep Learning Neural Network and other technologies to build an algorithm that takes an image of the eye with some other inputs and applies multiple algorithms to analyze if the eye in the image has cataract. This analysis may or may not be correct everytime. When performed on a test set, our algorithm has shown an accuracy of 94%. This tool is intended for screening and early detection of cataract purposes but is still under improvement and development. The report of this tool is not a final medical diagnosis.
